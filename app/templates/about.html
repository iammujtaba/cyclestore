About Supreme Cycle & Rickshaw Company

Your trusted partner for all cycling needs since 2020, serving the community with quality bicycles and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Supreme Cycle and Rickshaw Company started as a small bicycle shop in Mehdawal Road, Khalilabad with a simple mission: to provide quality bicycles and accessories to our community at affordable prices.

What began as a passion project has grown into a trusted destination for cycling enthusiasts, families, and professionals alike. We believe that cycling is not just a mode of transportation, but a way to live a healthier, more sustainable lifestyle.

Today, we continue to serve our customers with the same dedication and personal touch that started it all, now enhanced with our online presence to reach even more cycling enthusiasts.

Our Mission

To make cycling accessible to everyone by providing high-quality bicycles and accessories at competitive prices, backed by expert advice and outstanding customer service. We strive to promote a healthy and sustainable lifestyle through cycling.

Our Vision

To become the leading bicycle retailer in the region, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where cycling is the preferred choice for transportation and recreation.
