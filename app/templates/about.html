About Supreme Cycle & Rickshaw Company

Your trusted partner for all cycling needs since 1999, serving the community with quality bicycles and exceptional service for over 26 years.

Our Story

Established in 1999, Supreme Cycle & Rickshaw Company has been serving the community from our location on Mehdawal Road, Khalilabad-272175 for over 26 years. What started as a passion for providing quality bicycles has grown into one of the most trusted bicycle dealers in the region.

We specialize in a diverse range of cycling products including traditional bicycles, electric bicycles, cycle rickshaws, and BSA brand products. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us a 4.0-star rating from our valued customers.

As an authorized dealer and bicycle wholesaler, we serve both individual customers and retailers, offering delivery services, same-day delivery, and in-store collection options to meet all your cycling needs.

Today, we continue to serve our customers with the same dedication and personal touch that started it all, now enhanced with our online presence to reach even more cycling enthusiasts across the region.
